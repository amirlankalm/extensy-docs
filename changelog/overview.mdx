# Evolving Extensy

We are constantly improving the AI models and the developer experience for every builder in our community. Here's a look at the history of our platform.

## v1.0.0 — Initial Launch (March 2024)

Welcome to the future of Chrome extension development. Our first release brings the power of generative AI to anyone with an idea.

- **AI-powered MV3 Generation**: Describe your idea in natural language and receive a functional ZIP.
- **Manifest V3 by Default**: All generations follow Google's current standards for security and performance.
- **One-Click Download**: Instant packaging and delivery of your extension code.
- **Dashboard History**: Keep track of every extension you've ever generated.
- **Developer Credits**: A flexible system to power your iterations and refinements.

## Coming Soon

We're already working on the next generation of features based on your feedback.

- **Hot-Reload Support**: A custom tool to help you test local changes without manual reloads.
- **API Key Vault**: Securely store and manage your third-party keys (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) within our UI.
- **Theme Builder**: More control over the CSS and design of your generated popups.
- **Collaborative Projects**: Share your extension drafts with teammates for refinement.
