Welcome to **Extensy**, the AI-powered Chrome extension builder. In a world where browser workflows are increasingly personalized, coding an extension from scratch shouldn't be a bottleneck. 

Extensy allows you to describe exactly what you want in plain natural language and receive a fully functional, MV3-compliant, Chrome Web Store-ready extension as a downloadable ZIP in seconds.

## What makes Extensy different?

Unlike manual coding, template systems, or generic AI chatbots, Extensy is purpose-built for the Chrome extension lifecycle. It doesn't just give you code snippets; it architect's the entire extension structure, handles Manifest V3 compliance, and ensures every file is correctly linked and ready for the store.

## Quick Start

Getting your first extension live takes less than 5 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Create your account at [extensy.dev](https://extensy.dev) to get started with free credits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe Your Extension">
    Use our editor to describe what you want to build. Be as specific as you like!
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download ZIP">
    Once generated, download your extension as a ZIP file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Load in Chrome">
    Go to `chrome://extensions`, enable Developer Mode, and click "Load unpacked."
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What can you build?

The possibilities are virtually endless. Our users have built:

- **Productivity Tools**: Custom focus timers, tab managers, and task injectors.
- **Content Modifiers**: Ad-blockers, UI theme changers, and text highlight enhancers.
- **API Integrations**: Extensions that call OpenAI, Anthropic, or proprietary internal APIs.
- **Web Scrapers**: Tools that extract data from pages and save them to CSV or Google Sheets.
- **UI Injectors**: Adding sidebars, floating badges, or custom buttons to existing sites.
