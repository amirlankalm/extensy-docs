# More Info on Credits

Still have questions about how our generation pipeline uses your credits? This guide answers the most frequent inquiries from the Extensy community.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What exactly counts as one generation?">
    A generation is a complete AI pipeline run from prompt to ZIP output. If you build a new extension, that's one generation. If you use a refinement prompt to add a feature to an existing extension, that's also one generation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if my generation fails?">
    If the Extensy service encounters a technical error or your ZIP fails to build on our servers, your credit is immediately returned. Credits are only deducted for successful, downloadable outputs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do credits roll over to the next month?">
    No. Monthly credits are tied to your billing cycle. If you don't use your allotment, the credits will expire and reset at the start of your next billing period.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I buy extra credits without upgrading my plan?">
    Currently, the best way to get more credits is to upgrade to a higher plan. We are evaluating "pay-as-you-go" credit packs for future releases.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I share my credits with teammates?">
    Credits are currently tied to individual accounts. For team-based credit sharing, please contact us at [teams@extensy.dev](mailto:teams@extensy.dev) to discuss an enterprise-level agreement.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens to my credits if I downgrade?">
    If you downgrade your plan, you'll keep any remaining credits from your active PRO or MAX plan until the end of your billing cycle. After that, your balance will reset to the FREE plan limit.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund on unused credits?">
    We do not offer refunds for credits that were not used during your billing cycle. 
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do free plan credits reset monthly?">
    Yes. Every account is awarded 5 free credits on a monthly basis, starting from the day you sign up.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  **Pro-tip**: If you're building as a team, consider a single PRO account for your leads to iterate on the core extension structure before distributing the ZIP to other developers for manual polish.
</Tip>

<Note>
  **Important Note**: Credits are non-transferable between accounts. Ensure you're logged into the correct account before you click "Generate".
</Note>
