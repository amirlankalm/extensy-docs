# Choose Your Path to Building

Extensy is built to empower everyone, from casual hobbyists to professional agencies. Our plans are designed to scale with your needs as you build more complex browser extensions.

## A Plan for Every Builder

| Feature | **FREE** | **PRO** | **MAX** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Credits per month** | 5 | 50 | 250 |
| **Generation Speed** | Standard | High Priority | Instant |
| **Advanced Features** | Basic | Early Access | Early Access |
| **Commercial Rights** | Personal Use | Commercial Use | Commercial Use |
| **Priority Support** | No | Yes | 1-on-1 |

## The FREE Plan

Our entry-level plan is designed to help you get started with simple browser tools. 

- **5 Credits per month**: Generate up to 5 complete extensions or iterations each month.
- **Support**: Access to our community Discord server.
- **Usage**: Personal projects and learning.

## The PRO Plan

Perfect for solo developers, indie hackers, and professional builders who need more capacity and priority access.

- **50 Credits per month**: Substantial allowance for complex, iterative projects.
- **Priority Generation**: Your prompts skip the queue for faster turnaround.
- **Commercial Use**: Full rights to sell or monetize the extensions you build.

## The MAX Plan

Designed for teams and agencies that are building entire fleets of browser extensions or custom tools for clients.

- **250 Credits per month**: Enterprise-grade capacity for large-scale extension development.
- **Instant Generation**: Real-time response for all your prompts.
- **1-on-1 Support**: Direct access to the Extensy technical team for custom integration help.

## How to Upgrade

1. Log in to your [Extensy Dashboard](https://extensy.dev).
2. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**.
3. Choose your plan and follow the secure Stripe payment flow.

<Note>
  **Important Note**: Credits are awarded at the start of your billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-month, any remaining credits from your old plan will be added to your new balance!
</Note>

## Cancellation Policy

You can downgrade or cancel your subscription at any time. Your credits and pro features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

<Tip>
  **Need More?** If you have custom requirements that exceed our MAX plan, contact us at [sales@extensy.dev](mailto:sales@extensy.dev) for an enterprise-level agreement.
</Tip>
