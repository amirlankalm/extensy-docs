# Advanced Manifest Tweaks

While Extensy generates a valid `manifest.json` for you, some advanced browser features require manual keys. 

## Editing the Manifest

1. Download your extension ZIP.
2. Unzip the folder and open `manifest.json` in your editor.
3. Add your custom keys (e.g., `commands`, `web_accessible_resources`, etc.).

<Warning>
  After manual edits, the extension may no longer sync with the Extensy cloud. Always keep a backup!
</Warning>
