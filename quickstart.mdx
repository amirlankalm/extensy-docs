# Your First Extension

Follow these steps to generate, download, and test your first Chrome extension.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your First Tool">
    Navigate to the **Extensy Assistant** and type a prompt for your extension idea (e.g., "A tool that highlights price tags on any website").
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize and Refine">
    Wait for the AI to generate the `manifest.json` and `content.js`. Review the code and make any manual adjustments if necessary.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download the ZIP">
    Click the **Export** button in the top-right corner. This will package your extension files into a single, store-ready ZIP archive.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Unzip the Package">
    Locate the downloaded ZIP on your computer and extract its contents into a dedicated folder.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Load in Chrome">
    1. Navigate to `chrome://extensions`.
    2. Turn ON **Developer mode** in the top-right corner.
    3. Click the **Load unpacked** button.
    4. Select the folder you just unzipped.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Installation">
    Your extension card should now appear in the list. Pin the extension icon to your toolbar and test it on a webpage that matches your prompt!
  </Step>
</Steps>
