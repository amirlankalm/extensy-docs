# Your First Extension in Under 5 Minutes

Welcome to the Extensy fast track. This guide will walk you through the entire process of generating, downloading, and running your first AI-built Chrome extension.

## 01. Create an Account

Head over to [extensy.dev](https://extensy.dev) and sign up using your email or Google account. Once verified, you'll reach your dashboard where your journey begins.

## 02. Get Your Free Credits

On sign up, we automatically award you with free credits. These are enough to generate and iterate on a complete extension. 

<Info>
  Credits are only consumed on successful generation. If the builder runs into a technical error, your credit is immediately returned.
</Info>

## 03. Describe Your Extension

This is the fun part. In the text box, describe exactly what you want the extension to do. 

> **Example Prompt:** 
> "Build a Chrome extension that highlights every price on a webpage in green and shows a total sum found in a floating badge in the bottom-right corner."

## 04. Wait for Generation

Click "Generate." Behind the scenes, Extensy's AI pipeline constructs your `manifest.json`, background service worker, any necessary content scripts, and your popup's HTML, CSS, and JS. 

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the ZIP">
    Once the generation is done, click the **Download ZIP** button. Save the folder to a place you can easily access (like your Desktop or Downloads).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unzip the Folder">
    Extract the ZIP file so you have a regular folder containing the extension's code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Load in Chrome">
    Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Developer Mode">
    Toggle the switch in the top-right corner to turn on **Developer mode**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Load Unpacked">
    Click the **Load unpacked** button and select the unzipped folder you just created.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 05. Test Your Extension

Now, go to any webpage (e.g., [amazon.com](https://amazon.com) or [ebay.com](https://ebay.com)) and see your extension in action. If it's a content-modifier, it will automatically run. If it's a popup-tool, click the extension icon in your Chrome toolbar.

<Tip>
  If your extension isn't performing exactly how you'd like, don't worry. Head back to the Extensy dashboard, refine your prompt, and regenerate!
</Tip>

## What's Next?

Ready to build something more advanced? Check out our [Prompting Guide](/using-extensy/prompting-guide) to learn how to write high-performing prompts.

<Warning>
  **Privacy Matters**: Never share your extension's ZIP publicly until you've reviewed the `manifest.json` file for the permissions requested. Extensy tries to minimize permissions, but it's always good for you to know exactly what your extension is accessing.
</Warning>
