# Refining Your Creation

Building the perfect extension is an iterative process. Whether you're a non-coder using our AI or a developer fine-tuning the output, Extensy gives you two distinct paths to your goal.

## The Two Paths to Perfection

### 01. Re-prompting (AI-Powered)

If your extension is 80% of what you wanted, the easiest way to finish is to refine your prompt and regenerate.

**When to Re-prompt:**
- You want to add a feature (e.g., "now add a clear button").
- You want to change the UI (e.g., "make the popup blue instead of green").
- You want to target more websites (e.g., "also run on reddit.com").

**Example Refinement Prompt:**
> "The extension I just generated highlights every price on the page. Now, also show a floating total badge in the bottom-right corner that counts the number of prices found."

### 02. Manual Editing (Pro Mode)

If you're comfortable with a little bit of code, you can download the ZIP, unzip it, and modify the files yourself. 

**When to Edit Manually:**
- You want to use a specific CSS property the AI missed.
- You have your own custom logic or API keys you want to paste in.
- You want to add your own proprietary icons or assets.

## Manual Editing Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download and Unzip">
    Click the **Download ZIP** button from the Extensy dashboard and extract the folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open in VS Code">
    Open the unzipped folder in your favorite code editor (like VS Code).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit the Relevant File">
    Modify `popup.js`, `content.js`, or `manifest.json`. Save your changes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to chrome://extensions">
    Open Chrome and navigate to the extensions management page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reload the Extension">
    Click the **reload icon (↺)** on your extension's card.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Comparing the Two Approaches

| Feature | Re-prompting | Manual Editing |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Speed** | 10-30 seconds | Depends on your coding speed |
| **Skill Required** | None (natural language) | Basic HTML/CSS/JS |
| **Precision** | High (AI follows instructions) | Absolute (you control every byte) |
| **Cost** | 1 Credit | Free |

<Warning>
  **Important Note**: If you regenerate after making manual edits, your manual changes will be overwritten in the new ZIP. We recommend making all the AI-based refinements first, then downloading for final manual polish.
</Warning>

## Tips for Better Iterations

- **Be Incremental**: Don't try to add 10 features in one go. Add them one by one.
- **Reference What You Have**: "In the extension I just built, can you make the font larger?"
- **Test Frequently**: Load each version in Chrome and test it on real sites before moving on to the next task.
- **Mention Errors**: If your extension has a bug, describe what's happening: "The highlights appear, but they are overlapping with the image tags."

<Tip>
  Our AI is surprisingly good at debugging its own code. If something isn't working, just tell it the error message from the browser console!
</Tip>
